I’m Máximo Mussini

I’m a software engineer living in Montevideo, Uruguay. I like being close to the sea, and enjoy riding my bike by the shore.

You can find me on Twitter and GitHub. I love building stuff, so you should be able to find some cool stuff in the latter.

Would you like to chat? Feel free to email me, or if you have a question related to one of my open source projects open a GitHub issue so that others can read it as well. Subscribe to the blog for updates.
